About
Me

Hey there! My name is James, and I am an aspiring software developer! This is a journey that I have wanted to be on for a long time and I am grateful and excited to take the steps I am to achieving my goal by participating in a bootcamp. I got my first taste of coding somewhere around 14 years old from a Minecraft mod called computer craft. It allowed you to interact with computers in the world and program them to do things using the language lua. Since then, I have jumped into coding a few times on my own just finding my own resources, but having this opportunity to learn has already proven to be much more efficient.

I have the best motivation to keep me going through the camp. A wonderful wife and a sweet baby boy to take care of. I will do whatever is necessary to ensure they have the best life possible! Thankfully, this is something that I also enjoy! I am excited to continue to learn new skills during and after the bootcamp and to pursue a lifelong career coding.
